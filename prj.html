BIO

NOW — 2022 STORYTELLING

In 2024 Kolja finished studies on film direction in Catalyst Institute. During this time K. sharpened his writing and storytelling skills, directed few short films, developed storyboards, had a lot of experience with sound recording on set, worked on several festival short films. Since mid 2025 K. is currently expanding into game development — learning code (C#, Python, GDScript), working with Godot and Unity, and studying game design from the ground up. Writing narratives, designing characters, building prototypes.

2014 – 2022 ANIMATION & STORYBOARDS

Worked in a bar for a year to save enough money to start a career. Started doing standup. Made a pilot for animated show (“Carousel”). Joined a small animation studio, wrote and produced short animated clips. Kolja considers his first serious job at Rocket Fox — where he developed storyboards and animatics for Captain Kraken and Nibble Nom, animated series for children on Youtube and Netflix Kids. Kolja also spend a lot of time working in the writers' room, recording voice actors, directing musical scenes. Led a small (5+ people) project: an animated music video (link). In 2020 started working at MetraFilms as a storyboarder and composer, adding Premiere Pro to the toolkit alongside Adobe Animate, Toon Boom Harmony, Storyboard Pro, and Adobe Audition. In 2020 Completed CGMA 8week storyboard course. In 2021 moved from Moscow to Berlin.

1987 – 2014 EDUCATION

Started in Moscow, Russia. As a kid attended several art workshops, painted with hands and pens, had guitar and music theory in junior school, acted in school theatre. In high school K. family member got sick and K. had to care and support them until their departure in 2019. Kolja’s first summer job was a runner on a film set at Mosfilm Studios (Narrow Bridge, 2004). After school K. studied Foreign Languages in IEP University, left it on a fourth year to pursue art career. Played music in several bands across the town. In 2010 K. completed an animation program at College No. 60. Self-taught to digital drawing in Photoshop and tween animation in After Effects. Did various mini and freelance jobs: logo/brand design, animation, illustrations, drawing/preparing 2d assets for animation, interior design artist.
